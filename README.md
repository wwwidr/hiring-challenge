# Respaid Hiring Challenge

> **One challenge. One ticket. 48-hour decision.** Read it once, do it once, done.

We're hiring people who can build — not people who can pass a Laravel exam. This challenge tests the exact skill the job requires: **finding contactable decision-makers inside companies at scale**. It's our daily problem and our hardest problem.

## The ticket

Read **[tickets/TICKET-004-contact-finder.md](tickets/TICKET-004-contact-finder.md)**. That is the only ticket we evaluate right now. TICKET-001/002/003 are archived (the old Laravel challenge) — ignore them.

## The input

**[sample.csv](sample.csv)** — 50 real US companies, 25 SMBs + 25 mid-to-large brands, split across 3 tiers of input difficulty. Read the ticket for the schema.

## What you submit

1. A **private** GitHub repo with your runnable code + `output.csv` + `NOTES.md`.
2. A **screen recording with your camera on and your voice narrating** the full session. Face visible, no edits, max 60 minutes.
3. Add `wwwidr` as a collaborator on your private repo.
4. Reply to the thread / job listing with the repo link, the video link, and one sentence: "Camera was on during the full recording — confirmed."

Details, constraints, evaluation rubric, and the anti-fraud check are all in the ticket. Read it end-to-end before you start.

## How fast you'll hear back

- **48 hours from submission to yes/no.** Fast offers require trust upfront. The camera + voice narration is how we verify it's you.
- If the video quality doesn't match the code quality, we stop there. No drama, no drawn-out process.
- If it's strong, the next step is a 30-minute live call with our Chief of AI. If that goes well, we make an offer.

## What "good" looks like

- Creativity beats polish. A hacky, verified, high-coverage pipeline beats a clean one with generic answers.
- Per-tier strategies — SMB ≠ mid-large. A single playbook applied to both = no signal.
- Verification matters. An unverified contact ≠ a contact.
- AI is welcome. Show us *how* you drive it — your prompts, your rejections, your iterations. We want to see the operator, not the passenger.

## Why we do it this way

- Fast process (48h) shows we respect your time.
- Camera + voice is the cheapest way to filter real builders from AI-generated submissions. If that bothers you, this role isn't a fit.
- Real task, real data (no Leetcode, no algorithm trivia). Whatever you build could, in principle, ship to a client.

## Ground rules (read before you start)

- **$0 in paid APIs.** Free tiers only. The constraint is the test.
- **Your work must be in a PRIVATE repo.** Other candidates must not see it. Public forks are auto-declined.
- **Audio + camera are non-negotiable.** No audio = no camera = auto-decline. We don't watch silent screencasts.
- **One submission.** No iterative edits after you send. Get it right the first time.
